Our Mission

To promote equity & empathy and foster open conversations about American education.

The Open Doors Project is a research & art collective, founded by college students, high schoolers, and military personnel to understand the stengths & flaws in American high school education. Rates of depression and mental illness among young people are sharply increasing. Gen-Z is struggling, without the resources and support needed to be happy, healthy, successful. This is our documentation of the problems facing individual communities across the country. Join us in crafting our solution.

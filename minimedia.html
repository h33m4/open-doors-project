Featured Topics

is a collection of interviews featuring a diverse group of young adults regarding their experiences with purpose and its relation to the education they have received. In sharing our participants’ stories, ideas, and beliefs, we hope to learn more about the role that pre-college education systems in the United States play in the development of a student’s purpose.
